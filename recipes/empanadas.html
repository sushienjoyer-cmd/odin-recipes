Empanadas

Home

Delicious Argentinean pastries filled with seasoned meat and served with a side of chimichurri sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour and salt. Cut in the cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Add the milk and mix until a dough forms. Knead gently on a floured surface and let rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  4. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef with the onion and garlic until browned. Season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and cut into circles using a cookie cutter.
  6. Place a spoonful of filling on each circle and fold over to create a half-moon shape. Seal the edges with a fork.
  7. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry the empanadas until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until golden brown.
